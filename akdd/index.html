FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Is the APK safe to install?
Absolutely. Every release goes through VirusTotal scanning (72+ engines), SHA-256 code signing, and manual security review before publication. We never bundle adware, spyware, or unwanted software. You can verify the signature yourself using the hash published on our security page.
Why isn't the app on Google Play or App Store?
We're currently in a rapid iteration phase — shipping updates every 2 weeks with new features and improvements. Direct distribution lets us move faster without app store review delays. App store listings are planned for late 2026 once we hit our feature-complete milestone. Same app, same quality, just faster updates for you.
How do I install the APK on Android?
1. Tap the Download APK button above.
2. Open the downloaded .apk file.
3. If prompted, allow "Install from unknown sources" in Settings → Security.
4. Tap Install and you're done! The app will auto-update itself after that — no need to manually download future versions.
Is it really free? What's the catch?
Yes — 100% free. No hidden fees, no forced subscriptions, no paywalls on content. We're building our user base and believe that if we make the best streaming experience, growth follows. Premium features (like offline downloads and 4K) are also free during our launch period. Enjoy it while it lasts!
Which devices and OS versions are supported?
Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above for the APK. iOS 14+ for the IPA. Works on phones, tablets, Android TV, and Fire TV Stick. We recommend at least 2 GB of RAM for the best 4K streaming experience. Chromecast and AirPlay support are built in.
How do I get updates after installing?
The app has a built-in auto-updater. When a new version is available, you'll get a notification inside the app — just tap "Update" and it handles everything. No need to revisit this page or manually download again. We push updates every ~2 weeks with fixes and new features.
